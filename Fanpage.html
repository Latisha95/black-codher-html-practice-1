Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham is a Candian rapper, singer-songwriter and actor born October 24th 1986. He released his debut studio album,Thank Me Later in 2010. He is amongst the worlds best-selling music artist with over 170 million records sold. He has also won 4 Grammys Awards.
